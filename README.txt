To launch this do:
    1) Open Aconda Promt!!!
    2) >>>cd Elis
    3) >>>conda activate Elis
    4) >>>python launch.py

    Thats all!

To stop:
    Ctrl+C in terminal

To deactivate:
    >>>conda deactivate

    2) >>>cd Elis
    3) >>>conda activate yolov11_custom
